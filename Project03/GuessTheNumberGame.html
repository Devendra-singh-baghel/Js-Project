Guess The Number Game

User's Guesses :

🎮This game is called Guess the Number Game. You can predict the number that the computer generates at random in this game. You can take the following actions:

1. Make a guess.
2. Press the "Check" button.
3. After this you will get instructions whether your guess is bigger or smaller than the number generated by the computer.
4. Re-enter your guess in line with the instructions.
5. The game will end and your final score will be displayed if your guess matches the number that the generated by computer.
